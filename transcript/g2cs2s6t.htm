source 6
An exchange of letters between government officials and Monmouthshire Education Committee officials on providing spectacles for children 1908
(PRO ref: ED 125/11)
  

Source 6a: The request

MONMOUTHSHIRE EDUCATION COMMITTEE

COUNTY COUNCIL OFFICES
NEWPORT, MON

5th February 1908

EDUCATION (ADMINSTRATIVE PROVISIONS) ACT, 1907

Sir,
I beg to inform you that this Committee, at their last Meeting, decided to provide spectacles to School children suffering from defective eyesight in cases where parents are too poor to pay for them, and I am instructed to apply for your Board's approval, under Section 13(b) of the above Act, to this course.

I am, Sir

     

Your obedient Servant

    

(signed) JOHN W.HALL

    
 

Source 6b: The government's reply
 

…… the Board of Education are prepared to give their sanction under Section 13 (1) (b) of the Education (Administrative Provisions) Act, 1907 to the arrangements proposed by your Authority for providing spectacles to those children attending Public Elementary Schools whose parents are unable to pay for them.
