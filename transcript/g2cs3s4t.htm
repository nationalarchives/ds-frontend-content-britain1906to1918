Letter written to Lloyd George in 1914 calling for specialist old people's homes
(PRO ref: T 172/123)
  
Dear Sir,
May I appeal to you on behalf of the Aged to succeed and supplement the Old Age Pensions Acts by providing out of National Funds Old Age Homes for the destitute over 70 years of age? The figures enclosed show the great saving which would be effected by substituting Old Age Homes for the Workhouse. ……

Your promised Housing Scheme offers a fine opportunity of providing for aged as well as robust labourers at one stroke.

Earnestly commending the needs of the Homeless Aged to your heart and conscience/td>
I remain
Yours sincerely
F. Herbert Stead
