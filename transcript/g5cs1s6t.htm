A document showing the price of coal as determined by the government in March 1917
(PRO ref: POWE 26/8)		  

SCHEDULE OF PRICES.*
MONMOUTHSHIRE AND SOUTH WALES.

[Document lists various classes of coal with their prices]
*These prices are subject to an increase of 2s. 6d. per ton to meet the recent war wage increase, except in the case of shipments to France and Italy, and coal for the manufacture of patent fuel for shipment thereto.
