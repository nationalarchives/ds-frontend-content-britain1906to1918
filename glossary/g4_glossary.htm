This glossary is organised in alphabetical order. If you are looking for a word (e.g. Exchequer) it might be part of a phrase (e.g. Chancellor of the Exchequer).

Abstain Not do something
Affiliated Belonging to
Agitation Campaigning for something
Alienation Loss of friendship

Candour Accuracy or honesty
Chivalry / chivalrous Polite and respectful behaviour, usually towards women
Coerced Forced, or pushed around
Combatant Member of the armed forces involved in actual fighting
Constituency Area represented by an MP
Constitutional Allowed by law
Deplore Disapprove or dislike
Deputation Group who represent a larger group
Discharge Letting go
Enfranchise Give the vote
Expedient Right action at a particular time
Grading The rank held by someone in the armed forces
Handbills Small leaflets
Harboured Looked after someone
Ignominious Not very impressive
Impolicy Unwise political action
Indifferent Not interested
Initiating Starting
Inopportune Wrong action at the wrong time
Juncture Moment in time
Malice Dislike or hatred
Malignant Evil
Martyr Person who dies for a cause
Meted out Handed out
Metropolitan City area, sometimes referring specifically to London
Non-combatant Person not involved in actual fighting
Pious Religious
Profound Deep and moving
Recognisances Promises
Refrain Stop
Signatories People who have signed something (e.g. a letter or petition)

Tact Politeness
Undersigned People who have signed something (e.g. a letter or petition)
Vexation Anger
