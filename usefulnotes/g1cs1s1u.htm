Notes from a meeting of officials looking into the health of children in London schools 1903
(PRO ref: PC8/584)		  
  • This extract comes from the notes of a meeting of the Heads of the Boards of Education in London with some of the inspectors who looked at the health of children in the schools run by the Education Boards.
  • Dr Eicholz was a medical doctor. He was also medical inspector for the Education Board of the London Borough of Westminster, and some other London boroughs as well.
  • The meeting was connected with a government enquiry into physical deterioration which was set up in 1903. The enquiry was set up as a result of the poor condition of recruits for the Boer War which was fought 1899-1902 (look at the Background Information section for more details).
